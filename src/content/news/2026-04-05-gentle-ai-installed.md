Following the harness access shutdown, gentle-ai was installed as a lightweight CLI wrapper around the Anthropic API. It handles authentication, model routing, and streaming responses without depending on any third-party harness. The install confirmed that the api-direct strategy is viable for day-to-day development workflows within the HPS OS.
