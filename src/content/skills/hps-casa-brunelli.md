Casa Brunelli is the Tuscan villa short-term rental business. This skill enforces the seasonal pricing model — high season (June–September), shoulder (April–May, October), low season (November–March) with distinct nightly rates. Stripe handles payments via SEPA Direct Debit for European guests. Docuseal generates rental contracts from templates with dynamic clause injection. The cleaning schedule syncs with checkout dates. The admin dashboard follows a calendar-first layout with occupancy heatmaps.
