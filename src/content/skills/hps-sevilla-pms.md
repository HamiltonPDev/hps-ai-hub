Sevilla PMS is the property management system for Hamilton's Seville rental portfolio — 6 apartments, 24 rooms. This skill embeds the LAU (Ley de Arrendamientos Urbanos) rental logic: deposit rules, contract renewal terms, rent update indices. The tenant regularization workflow handles the transition from informal to formal contracts. Maintenance requests follow a ticket-based flow with priority escalation. Monthly income calculations account for variable occupancy, utility splits, and tax withholdings.
