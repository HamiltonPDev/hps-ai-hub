Fiscal compliance spans Spanish and Dutch tax regimes for Hamilton's BV structure. This skill covers IRPF Modelo 100 filing for Spanish rental income, the 50% deduction for rental expenses under Spanish law, and the Spain-NL bilateral tax treaty (Article 12 — royalties and immovable property). The Dutch side handles e-boekhouden invoice structure for the BV and NL BTW (VAT) rules. Sevilla's zona tensionada designation affects rent cap calculations. All tax logic is advisory — the skill flags patterns, not computes final amounts.
